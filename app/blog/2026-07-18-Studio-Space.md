For many years I have hoped to move. As a child we moved so many times. My M.O. is to stay in one place for a long time. I planned to break that pattern but I've lived in the same place for longer than I'd like to admit.

For years I harped about getting out. It is expensive to move. Now housing in New Your City is crazy. Last year I saw many friends live in Air B&Bs because they couldn't find any apartment.

I've now embarked on a new career as an artist. After 40 years of graphic design, copywriting, and web design, I have an opportunity to make art. Artists need space. I am lucky to have ample space.

This year I am organizing my work space. That entails getting rid of clutter and the junk I bring home. I need to lighten the load if I am ever to get out of here.

Problems persist, Rent has risen twice over the last two years. Today a leak started in the ceiling after a rainstorm. As I focus on making art I find that I need organized space.

I have a digital vision board of who is &ldquo;The Man I'be Always Wanted to Be&rdquo;. In it I imagine the life I want to live including my fabulous housing and art studio.

This is a status report on The Man I am Being and a look at my current workspace.

<section style="display:block; clear:both;">
<h2>The Sun Room</h2>

The house has an addition attached to the back of the building. This used to be my bedroom. A pipe broke in the basement heating system so there is no longer any heat in winter. The basement also was flooded in hurricane Ida. So I moved upstairs leaving this room free. I have a long table here where I will work with paper and framing. Right now completed artwork is stored on the table. I intend to build an elevated storage area under the table to move the art and clear the table.

<section style="display:block; clear:both; ">
<h2>The Outdoor Wood Shop</h2>

As I continue to paint and draw completed artworks are beginning to pile up. I need to frame art to protect it and for show. Recently a friend loaned a table-top saw to me. I was shopping for a table-top saw and studying which to buy. 

My friend showed me where the local lumber yard is. I bought maple wood and I have begun to make picture frames. When I cut wood I bring the saw into the yard to work. Otherwise the saw is stored in the sun room.
